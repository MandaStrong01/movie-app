Uploading Content

Page 10: Doxy The School Bully

This page is specifically designed for uploading your existing movie files.

Upload Process:

  1. Click the "Upload Video" button
  2. Select one or multiple video files from your device
  3. Wait for the upload to complete
  4. Your videos will appear in the video selection bar
  5. Click any video thumbnail to preview it
  6. Use the playback controls (Play/Pause, Skip Back/Forward)

Supported Formats: Most common video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, and more.

Media Box

Page 11: Editor Dashboard

The Media Box is your central hub for all uploaded and AI-generated assets.

Upload Options:

  • Open Files: Upload images, videos, audio, PDFs, documents
  • Open Photos/Videos: Quick access to your device's photo library
  • Open Google Drive: Connect your Google Drive account for cloud uploads
  • Drag & Drop: Simply drag files into the Media Box area

Asset Management:

  • All your uploaded files and AI-generated content appear here
  • Click any asset to preview it in the Viewer
  • Delete unwanted assets using the X button (appears on hover)
  • Assets are automatically organized by date

Important Reminder: Before moving to the next step, make sure you've uploaded all necessary assets. Look for the "Have I Missed A Thing?" reminder to double-check!

Editing Tools

Pages 12-16: Professional Editing Suite

These pages provide comprehensive editing capabilities for your film.

Key Features:

  • Timeline Editor: 4-track system (SRT, VIDEO, AUDIO, TEXT)
  • Duration Control: Set your film length (up to your plan's maximum)
  • Aspect Ratio: Choose 16:9, 4:3, 1:1, or custom ratios
  • Resolution: Select from 720p, 1080p, 4K options
  • Volume Control: Adjust audio levels
  • AI Duration Calculator: Smart duration estimation
  • Generate Function: Compile your edited film

Editing Workflow:

  1. Select assets from your Media Box
  2. Adjust settings (duration, ratio, size, volume)
  3. Preview changes in real-time
  4. Use the Generate button to process your edits
  5. All generated outputs save automatically to Media Box

Timeline

4-Track System

  1. SRT Track: Subtitles and closed captions
  2. VIDEO Track: Your video content and visual effects
  3. AUDIO Track: Music, dialogue, sound effects
  4. TEXT Track: Title cards, credits, overlays

Working with the Timeline:

  • Drag assets from Media Box onto tracks
  • Arrange clips in sequence
  • Trim and adjust clip lengths
  • Layer multiple elements
  • Maximum duration: 180 minutes (3 hours) for Studio plan

Tips for Success

  1. Start Small: Familiarize yourself with the interface before tackling large projects
  2. Explore AI Tools: Try different tools to discover what works best for your style
  3. Organize Assets: Keep your Media Box organized by deleting unused files
  4. Save Often: Your work auto-saves, but generate checkpoints regularly
  5. Preview Frequently: Check your work in preview mode throughout the editing process
  6. Use Drag & Drop: It's the fastest way to upload and organize files
  7. Check the Guide: Reference this guide whenever you need clarification

Troubleshooting

Common Issues:

Google Drive Not Loading:

  • Ensure you've granted necessary permissions
  • Try using regular upload instead
  • Check your internet connection

Upload Failed:

  • Verify file format is supported
  • Check file size (larger files may take longer)
  • Try compressing images before upload

Video Not Playing:

  • Ensure video format is compatible
  • Check browser compatibility (Chrome recommended)
  • Clear browser cache and reload
